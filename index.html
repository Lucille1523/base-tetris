How It Works

Connect Wallet

Link your MetaMask to BASE network. Ultra-low gas fees, lightning-fast transactions — you'll barely notice the chain.

Each game costs 10 bTetris — burned immediately on entry. Every game session permanently reduces the total supply.

By Design

Supply Only Goes Down

🔥

Transfer Burns

Every single token transfer incurs a 0.25% permanent burn. Every wallet-to-wallet, every DEX trade — reducing supply automatically.

🎮

Entry Fee Burns

10 bTetris is destroyed upon each game session start. The more players, the faster the supply shrinks.

📊

Score Submission Burns

An additional burn mechanism is triggered on every score submission, layering deflationary pressure on top of gameplay.

🚫

No Minting. Ever.

Fixed supply at 20,000,000. Minting is permanently disabled at the contract level. Supply can only decrease — never increase.
